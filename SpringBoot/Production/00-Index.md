## What you should be able to answer after Production
- How authentication, authorization, CSRF, CORS, and method security fit together.
- How to expose health, metrics, logs, and traces safely.
- How to think about caching, retries, circuit breakers, timeouts, and thread pools.
- How Boot apps are packaged, configured, deployed, and operated.
